KASAYSAYAN NG BARANGAY VI-D

Barangay VI-D, also known as Poblacion, is a barangay located in San Pablo City, Laguna, within the CALABARZON region of the Philippines. It is one of the smaller barangays in terms of population, having 1,531 residents as of the 2020 census, which represents about 0.54% of the city's total population​​. San Pablo City itself has a rich history, being one of the oldest cities in the Philippines, with its roots tracing back to pre-Spanish times when it was known as Sampalok. It was named San Pablo de los Montes during Spanish colonization in honor of Saint Paul the First Hermit. San Pablo is famously known as the "City of Seven Lakes" due to its numerous natural lakes, which are significant both culturally and economically​. Barangay VI-D's history and development are closely tied to the broader historical context of San Pablo City. The barangay's demographic data over the years indicate a fluctuating population, with a notable decrease from 1,874 in 1990 to 1,531 in 2020​ (PhilAtlas)​​ (City Population)​. The area is primarily residential with some local businesses and services catering to its residents​.
