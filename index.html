Our Services

Explore Our PROGRAMS

EDUCATIONAL ASSISTANCE

The Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD) assistance program allows students who are in crisis to get a financial grant. Beneficiaries will depend on the student's school level and qualifications and they may receive between PHP 1000 to PHP 4000 through the AICS program.

FEEDING PROGRAM

The School-Based Feeding Program (SBFP) by Konsehala Jinkee is one intervention to address undernutrition of school children. The Philippine Department of Education (DepEd) partners with various organizations to implement the SBFP
FREE MEDECINES

Per guidelines, those who want to avail of free medicines should provide a valid ID, proof of indigency such as Barangay Certificate or Certification issued by the medical social service, and the doctor's prescription, which should have been issued in the past three (3) months.
SPORTS LEAGUE

A group of individual athletes, sports teams or clubs who form a league to compete against each other and gain points in a specific sport.
FREE HAIRCUT

The primary objective of “Libreng Gupit” is to make grooming services accessible to those who may not have the means to afford them. By providing free haircuts, the project aims to empower individuals and help them feel more confident and presentable.
FREE PRINTING SERVICE

Free printing service from our barangay Konsehal, Jap Panergayo. This project was introduced to students here in our barangay with regard to their school projects.
